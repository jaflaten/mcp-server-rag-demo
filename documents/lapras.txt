Lapras is a Water/Ice-type Pokemon with a gentle nature that loves to carry people across water. It has high intelligence and can understand human speech. Lapras is known for its beautiful singing voice and its ability to travel across seas while carrying passengers on its back. It was once nearly hunted to extinction but has since recovered in numbers. Lapras can create ice paths on water and has a tough shell that protects its soft body. It appears to enjoy ferrying people across bodies of water.
