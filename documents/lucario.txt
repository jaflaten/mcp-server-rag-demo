Lucario is a Fighting/Steel-type Pokemon with the ability to sense and manipulate aura. It can read the thoughts and feelings of others by sensing their aura waves. Lucario evolves from Riolu when leveled up with high friendship during the day. It is known for its sense of justice and loyalty to its trainer. Lucario can Mega Evolve into Mega Lucario, which increases its attack power significantly. The spikes on its hands and chest are used to detect and manipulate aura.
