Tyranitar is a Rock/Dark-type Pokemon with incredible destructive power. It is so strong it can change the landscape by walking, causing mountains to crumble. Tyranitar evolves from Pupitar at level 55 and is the final evolution of Larvitar. Its armor-like hide can withstand any attack, and it fears no opponent. Tyranitar can Mega Evolve into Mega Tyranitar, which has even thicker armor and greater power. It is known as an extremely aggressive Pokemon that will challenge anyone.
