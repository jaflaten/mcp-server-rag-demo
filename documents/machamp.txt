Machamp is a Fighting-type Pokemon with four powerful arms that can throw 1,000 punches in two seconds. Its muscles never tire, allowing it to fight continuously. Machamp evolves from Machoke when traded and is the final evolution of Machop. It can move mountains with its strength and knows every martial arts technique. Each of its four arms has incredible power and can strike from different angles simultaneously. Machamp wears a power-save belt to regulate its immense strength.
