Mewtwo is a Psychic-type Legendary Pokemon created through genetic manipulation of Mew's DNA. It is considered one of the most powerful Pokemon in existence. Mewtwo has incredible psychic abilities and can levitate using its mind. It was created by scientists who wanted to make the ultimate Pokemon. Mewtwo can Mega Evolve into Mega Mewtwo X or Mega Mewtwo Y, each with different stat distributions. Its cold, glowing eyes strike fear into its enemies.
