Alakazam is a Psychic-type Pokemon with an extraordinarily high IQ of over 5,000. Its brain cells multiply continuously throughout its life, making it smarter with age. Alakazam evolves from Kadabra when traded and is the final evolution of Abra. It remembers everything it has ever experienced and can perform complex calculations instantly. Alakazam can Mega Evolve into Mega Alakazam, which has five spoons instead of two. It uses its spoons to amplify its psychic powers.
