Blaziken is a Fire/Fighting-type Pokemon with powerful legs that can leap over 30-story buildings. It attacks with scorching kicks and punches, with flames emerging from its wrists and ankles. Blaziken evolves from Combusken at level 36 and is the final evolution of Torchic. It has a unique ability called Speed Boost that increases its speed every turn. Blaziken can Mega Evolve into Mega Blaziken, making it even faster and more powerful. Its fighting style resembles kickboxing and martial arts.
