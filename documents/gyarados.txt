Gyarados is a Water/Flying-type Pokemon known for its fierce temperament and destructive power. It evolves from the weak Magikarp at level 20, representing one of the most dramatic transformations in Pokemon. Gyarados can create storms and destroy entire cities when enraged. Its serpentine body can reach over 20 feet in length. Despite being part Flying-type, Gyarados cannot learn Fly naturally. It can Mega Evolve into Mega Gyarados, which becomes Water/Dark-type and even more aggressive.
