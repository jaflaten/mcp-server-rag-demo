Pikachu is an Electric-type Pokemon known for its yellow fur and red cheeks. It can generate powerful electric attacks and stores electricity in its cheek pouches. Pikachu is one of the most popular Pokemon and is often seen as the mascot of the franchise. It evolves from Pichu when leveled up with high friendship and can evolve into Raichu when exposed to a Thunder Stone. Pikachu's tail is shaped like a lightning bolt, and it can be used to sense danger.
