Dragonite is a Dragon/Flying-type Pokemon known for its kind nature despite its powerful appearance. It can fly around the globe in just 16 hours and is capable of rescuing drowning people from stormy seas. Dragonite evolves from Dragonair at level 55, completing the Dratini evolution line. It has small wings compared to its large body but is an excellent flier. Dragonite is intelligent enough to understand human speech and has been known to guide lost ships to safety.
