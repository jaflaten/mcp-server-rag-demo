Gengar is a Ghost/Poison-type Pokemon that hides in shadows and drops room temperatures by 10 degrees Fahrenheit. It is mischievous and loves to play pranks on people by pretending to be their shadow. Gengar evolves from Haunter when traded and is the final form of Gastly. It can hide perfectly in the shadow of any object and is said to steal the life of those who are lost in mountains. Gengar can Mega Evolve into Mega Gengar, which can trap opponents and prevent them from escaping.
